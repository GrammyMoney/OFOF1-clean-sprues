# How to assemble an OFOF1 (regular version)

## Step 1: Tools

Here is the bare minimum of what you will need:
 - Soldering iron
 - Solder
 - Flush cutters
 - Hobby knife
 - 3mm hex key

A multimeter is not technically required but will make your life easier
